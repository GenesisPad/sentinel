# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Crypto users evaluating unfamiliar EVM tokens and Genesis Sentinel operators monitoring product usage. Users need fast, trustworthy risk evidence in Telegram and on the web without needing to interpret raw contract data.

## Product Purpose

Genesis Sentinel turns persisted on-chain evidence, simulations, liquidity data, holder analysis, and contract findings into clear token-risk intelligence. Success means users understand detected risks and evidence gaps without the product ever claiming that a token is safe.

## Brand Personality

Precise, evidence-first, and security-professional. Calm enough to inspire trust, direct enough to make serious risks unmissable, and concise enough for time-sensitive crypto workflows.

## Anti-references

Avoid noisy degen dashboards, decorative chart clutter, unexplained metrics, misleading green “safe token” claims, fear-driven copy, and common token-bot patterns that present missing evidence as a clean result.

## Design Principles

1. Evidence before verdict: connect every conclusion to persisted observations and expose uncertainty.
2. Make danger unmistakable: critical findings must lead, not hide beneath routine metrics.
3. Compress without distorting: Telegram summaries should be dense but never ambiguous.
4. Familiar controls, clear outcomes: filters, refreshes, and navigation should behave predictably.
5. Restrict operational intelligence: admin analytics must be enforced server-side and explain access denial.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use high contrast, legible labels, and redundant visual encoding so charts never rely on color alone. Prefer direct dates and values over unexplained shorthand. Keep controls usable in Telegram clients with reduced motion or limited screen space.
